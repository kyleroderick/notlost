Listening

January 16, 2015

This is a constantly updated list of podcasts I’m listening to. Podcasts are listed alphabetically.

  • Accidental Tech Podcast: Three nerds discussing tech, Apple, programming, and loosely related matters.
  • Better Know a Jackal: A weekly interview with fans of podcasting.
  • Connected: A weekly panel discussion on Apple and the impact of technology on our lives.
  • Design Details: A weekly show about the people who design our favorite products.
  • Design Review: A weekly podcast about products from a unique designer perspective.
  • The Dirt: Pugilistic UX at its best.
  • DLC: Weekly audio tour of the latest in video games and tabletop entertainment.
  • Eat This Podcast: Talking about anything around food
  • Ghosts and Echoes: An audio companion to the game Destiny.
  • The Incomparable: A weekly dive into geeky media we love.
  • Inquisitive: A show for the naturally curious.
  • Nerds on Draft: Craft beer and tech. My bread and butter.
